Find Out More

Hi! I'm Zachary Koehn.

I'm an aspiring game designer and producer by day, normal man by night.

Currently a senior in software engineering at Iowa State University, this leg of my education will be ending in the spring of 2017. I've learned to code, but my passion and natural talent lies in user experience and project management.

As for passion, anyone that knows me will tell you that it lies with video games. I love the idea of creating worlds and stories that hook people into them and leave lasting impressions. But mostly, I love that more and more people are playing games and sharing in my favorite pasttime.

With this boom, more and more people are looking to enter the industry as a career option. But right now, we just aren't ready to accept these people in the industry.

That's why I aim to educate as many people as possible about what it's like in the industry and how they can work towards entry into the industry. How do I aim to do this? By educating people in the Midwest and setting an example by starting my own studio here that makes games that matter. It's a daunting task, I know, but I have the right team for it and we're going to try it. Who knows. Maybe we're just the greater fools that think we can make it where others have failed.

But then again, game design used to be founded on greater fools.

I am the president of ISU_GDC, Iowa State's game development club. I run the meetings, organize the executives, and guide the club to the greatness that we all see in it.

I've gotten us grants for high-end streaming equipment, so that we can stream our meetings and game breakdowns.

I've organized travel plans to bring important lecturers to talk about game design.

But I do this all because I live for ISU_GDC.
